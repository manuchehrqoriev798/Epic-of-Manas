Timeline of Epic Manas

A chronological journey through the legendary epic

Follow the chronological progression of events through all three parts of the Epic Manas, from the birth of Manas to the reign of his grandson Seytek.

Part 1: Manas

Birth of Manas

The miraculous birth of Manas to Jakip and Chiyirdi, marked by supernatural signs and prophecies of his future greatness.

Part 1: Manas

Early Signs of Greatness

Young Manas shows extraordinary strength and leadership qualities, defeating adult warriors in combat by age nine.

Part 1: Manas

Unification of Kyrgyz Tribes

Manas unites the forty Kyrgyz tribes, establishing the first Kyrgyz state and becoming their supreme leader.

Part 1: Manas

Marriage to Kanykei

Manas marries the wise and beautiful Kanykei, who becomes his trusted advisor and the mother of Semetey.

Part 1: Manas

The Great Campaign

Manas leads his army on the Great Campaign, defending Kyrgyz lands and establishing alliances with neighboring peoples.

Part 2: Semetey

Birth of Semetey

Birth of Semetey to Manas and Kanykei, followed by Manas's death and Kanykei's flight to Bukhara with the infant.

Part 2: Semetey

Return to Talas

Semetey returns to his homeland to reclaim his father's legacy and restore justice to the Kyrgyz people.

Part 2: Semetey

Battles and Triumphs

Semetey fights numerous battles to defend his people and faces betrayal from within his own ranks.

Part 3: Seytek

Early Life of Seytek

After Semetey's disappearance, young Seytek is protected and raised in secret by loyal supporters.

Part 3: Seytek

Fulfillment of Legacy

Seytek completes the mission begun by his grandfather Manas, establishing lasting peace and prosperity for the Kyrgyz people.
